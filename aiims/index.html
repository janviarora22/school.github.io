DIRECTOR's MESSAGE

Dear Students,

I feel immensely elated to have been given the responsibility by the Management of Arya Samaj, Model Town of running the most prestigious educational institution of the state. The aim of education, in the present era, is not only to prepare the students to earn their livelihood but also to prepare them to face the challenges, realities and complexities of life. To achieve these, education and hard work certainly goes a long way.  Therefore, you have to make sure you work even harder and really dedicate yourself to what you are doing and setting out to achieve. Life is a game with a glorious prize if you learn to play it right. To find diamonds and gold, you have to dig deep, sweat hard and search long.

Great minds have great goals and purposes; not wishes. If you look at all great and miraculous feats of great minds which are looked upon with awe, praise and wonder, you will find their lives full of enthusiasm, dedication and perseverance.

School, as life itself, teaches you a variety of things. There are ample opportunities of ideas, initiations and innovations. Being in school is largely a creative experience. Each day brings new learning and more chances to participate in various activities. You should try and take full advantage of these opportunities. If you have a dream to take a new flight into that limitless sky; determine, spread your wings and take off leaving behind the fear of being a failure.

I, here, need the trust and co-operation of the parents and guardians to shape up your children to be creative, optimistic and progressive citizens of the natio to take the country to new heights.

M.L. Kalra (Director Academics)
